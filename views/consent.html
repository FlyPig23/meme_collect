Research Study Consent Form

Research Purpose 研究目的

You are invited to participate in a research study about cross-cultural understanding of internet memes. This study aims to investigate how people from different cultural backgrounds interpret and understand memes. 诚邀您参与一项关于跨文化网络表情包理解的研究。本研究旨在探究来自不同文化背景的人如何理解和诠释网络表情包。

Procedure 研究程序

If you agree to participate, you will: 如果您同意参与，您将：

  • Complete a brief demographic questionnaire 完成一份简短的人口统计问卷
  • Upload at least 10 memes that you are familiar with and have cultural significance
  • Provide detailed "Cultural Context" explanations for each meme, and "Potential Misunderstandings" (you can use either Chinese or English based on your preference)
  • Label each meme with:
    • A sentiment label (choose one: positive, negative, or neutral)
    • Emotion labels (can select multiple: sarcastic, humorous, motivation, offensive)
  • Complete a brief demographic questionnaire 完成一份简短的人口统计问卷
  • Review and validate 20 meme interpretations from different cultural perspectives
  • Rate the accuracy of cultural context explanations and potential misunderstandings
  • Provide feedback on the interpretations to help improve cross-cultural understanding if you have any thoughts
  • Complete a brief demographic questionnaire 完成一份简短的人口统计问卷
  • Review 20 memes from US culture 查看20个来自美国文化的网络表情包
  • For each meme: 对于每个网络表情包：
    • Select the correct meme interpretation from three options 从三个选项中选择正确的表情包的解释
    • Choose a sentiment label (positive, negative, or neutral) 选择一个情感标签（积极的、消极的或中性的）
    • Select emotion labels that best describe the meme (can choose multiple: sarcastic, humorous, motivation, offensive) 选择最能描述这个表情包的情绪标签（可多选：讽刺的、幽默的、激励的、冒犯的）
  • Your responses will help us understand how people from different cultural backgrounds interpret memes 您的回答将帮助我们理解来自不同文化背景的人如何理解网络表情包

Data Usage 数据使用

We would like to use the data we obtain in this study for current research as well as future studies. These studies may provide additional information that will be helpful in understanding cross-cultural communication and developing better tools for cultural exchange online. 我们希望将在本研究中获得的数据用于当前研究以及未来的研究。 这些研究可能会提供额外的信息，有助于理解跨文化交流并开发更好的在线文化交流工具。

Confidentiality 保密性

Your responses will be anonymous. No personally identifiable information will be collected or stored. All data will be securely stored and used only for research purposes. 您的回答将是匿名的。不会收集或存储任何可识别个人身份的信息。所有数据将被安全存储，仅用于研究目的。

Risks and Benefits 风险和收益

There are no anticipated risks beyond those encountered in daily life. Some memes may contain mild humor or cultural references. Your participation will help improve cross-cultural understanding of internet content and contribute to academic research in digital communication. 预计不会有超出日常生活的风险。部分网络梗可能包含轻微的幽默或文化引用。您的参与将有助于提高对网络内容的跨文化理解，并为数字传播领域的学术研究做出贡献。

Voluntary Participation 自愿参与

Your participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty. You may skip any questions you feel uncomfortable answering. 您的参与是自愿的。您可以随时退出，不会受到任何惩罚。如果对某些问题感到不适，您可以选择跳过。
